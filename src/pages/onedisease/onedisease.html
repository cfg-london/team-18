Summary

ALL is a type of cancer that happens when your bone marrow (the spongy stuff inside your bones) isn’t working properly.

Leukaemia will replace your healthy blood cells with ones that don’t work properly.

 Treatment

You will have chemotherapy. These medicines are to kill the cancer cells and help your bone marrow to work normally. The treatment course lasts for a long time - it continues even when you don’t feel ill anymore, to ensure the leukaemia doesn't come back.

Sometimes you will also need to have a bone marrow or stem cell transplant, but this depends on your leukaemia, so check with your doctor.

 Tips

Chemotherapy might make you feel more tired and poorly than usual. It's good idea to try to stay in the best shape possible to negate it.

Also try to keep yourself warm, rested and infection-free - let your body concentrate on fighting the disease!
